How your 7‑day reset works.

  1. Day 1 — Clear a little space. A short reflection and one small environment tweak to make the rest of the week easier.
  2. Day 2 — Calm your inputs. A simple boundary around notifications, news, or scrolling so your brain can exhale.
  3. Day 3 — Move gently. No performance, no tracking — just a short, kind movement block that feels doable.
  4. Day 4 — Anchor your evenings. A small evening ritual that signals to your body that the day is allowed to end.
  5. Day 5 — Choose one thing that matters. A focused, realistic win that reminds you that you can still move something important forward.
  6. Day 6 — Restore your attention. A short practice to bring your attention back from autopilot and into the present.
  7. Day 7 — Close the loop. A gentle review and a small promise to your future self, so this week does not just blur and vanish.

Why this kind of reset actually works.

Peakora does not try to rebuild your entire life in a week. It focuses on a few levers that quietly change how your days feel:

  • Attention. What you notice and what you ignore shapes how heavy your week feels.
  • Energy. Small, consistent actions beat heroic bursts that leave you more exhausted.
  • Environment. Tiny changes to your surroundings make better choices feel less effortful.
  • Closure. Weeks that end with reflection feel less like a blur and more like progress.

The plan is simple on purpose. You do not need more complexity. You need a week that feels a little kinder to live inside.
