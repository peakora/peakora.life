Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what information Peakora collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Our goal is to keep things simple, transparent, and respectful.

1. Information You Provide

When you start the 7‑day reset or contact us through the Peakora agent, you may share:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address
  • Any message or question you submit

This information is used only to deliver your plan, reply to your message, or provide support.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, basic technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • General usage data

This helps us understand how people use Peakora and improve the experience.

3. How Your Information Is Used

Your information may be used to:

  • Send your 7‑day plan
  • Respond to your questions
  • Improve the Peakora experience

4. How Your Information Is Protected

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no online service can guarantee perfect security.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your information is only shared when necessary to operate Peakora or comply with legal requirements.

6. Your Choices

You can request to:

  • Access the information you provided
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your information

To make a request, simply contact us through the Peakora agent or the email provided in your plan.

7. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, you can reach out through the Peakora agent or reply to any email you receive from us.
